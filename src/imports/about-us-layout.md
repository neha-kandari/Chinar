# ABOUT US SECTION LAYOUT

## 1. Section Header

Large background text (light opacity)

**ABOUT US**

Small tag above title
`• ABOUT BEST ROADWAYS`

**Heading**

**Driving Logistics Excellence Since 1985**

Short intro paragraph:

Best Roadways Limited has been a trusted name in transportation and logistics for over four decades. Founded in 1985 by Mr. Jaikumar Gupta with a single truck in Bombay, the company has grown into one of India’s leading logistics providers with a strong fleet, advanced infrastructure, and nationwide service network.

# 3. Journey Timeline (Clean Horizontal Timeline)

Instead of showing every year, highlight **major milestones only**.

### 1985

Company founded by Mr. Jaikumar Gupta in Bombay with a single truck.

### 1990

Introduced the 36-hour express delivery model for time-bound cargo.

### 1995

Constructed one of the largest distribution hubs at JNPT.

### 2000

Annual turnover crossed ₹100 Crores.

### 2005

Fleet size crossed 250 vehicles.

### 2009

Turnover crossed ₹200 Crores and expanded bonded warehousing.

### 2016

Introduced digital driver ID cards and cashless driver transactions.

### 2018

Fleet strength crossed 750 vehicles.

### 2021

Achieved positive growth despite the COVID-19 pandemic.

### 2022

Recognized as Logistics Company of the Year by TV9 Network.

### 2023

Turnover projected to cross ₹500 Crores.
