# 🧱 STRUCTURE – One Section, Soft Differentiation

Instead of hard left-right split, use this:

```
---------------------------------------------------
        SECTION TITLE + SUBTEXT
---------------------------------------------------

    [ LOCAL BUSINESSES WE POWER ]
    (small description)
    ───────────────────────────
    LOGO GRID (compact)

    (subtle divider line or spacing)

    [ TRUSTED BY GROWING & NATIONAL BRANDS ]
    (small description)
    ───────────────────────────
    LOGO GRID (slightly larger & spaced)
---------------------------------------------------
```

Everything stays inside **one background container**.

---

# 🎨 How To Make It Feel Like ONE Section

## 1️⃣ Same Background

Use:

* White background
  OR
* Very light grey (#f8f9fa)

No background change between them.

---

## 2️⃣ Soft Divider (Not Harsh)

Between the two groups:

Option A:
Thin 1px green line (#00A54F at 20% opacity)

Option B:
Large vertical spacing (80px–100px)
No visible divider, just breathing space

This keeps flow intact.

---

## 3️⃣ Visual Hierarchy Difference (Subtle)

### Local Clients Grid

* 5–6 logos per row
* Slightly smaller logo size
* Tighter spacing
* Categories below in small muted text

Feels:
Community-focused
Strong local presence

---

### National Brands Grid

* 3–4 logos per row
* Larger logo containers
* More padding
* Slight elevation effect

Feels:
Premium
Established
